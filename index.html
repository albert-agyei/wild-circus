About Us

Our troop was born in 1994, the first 10 years were a real challenge, we had to fight to make a name and have our place in this business. Thanks to our founder who made us who we are today, a world-renowned circus. We only performed in Ghana during these first 10 years. Wild circus is a special kind of entertainment show that can be enjoyed by children and adults. Our performances include acrobats, clowns, trained animals, trapeze acts, musicians, hoopers, tightrope walkers, jugglers and other artists who perform stunts.
